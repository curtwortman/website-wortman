# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - website-wortman

## Product Vision & Scope
**Goal**: Replicate the exact final webpage aesthetics from the YouTube demo - dark cosmic theme, glassmorphism UI, 3D tilt effects, shooting star background, MacOS-style cards, infinite testimonial scroll, and secure admin overlay.

## Target Audience
- Professional peers in the semiconductor and AI industry.
- Potential partners and stakeholders.
- Enterprise customers seeking technical advisory.

## MVP Definition
- Fully responsive 3D landing page.
- Particles.js cosmic background.
- Glassmorphic project/innovation cards.
- Integrated Keycloak authentication for restricted sections.

## Success Metrics
- Performance: < 2s initial load time.
- Visuals: "Pixel-perfect" alignment with the 3D Cosmic theme.
- Animation Frame Rate: Stable 60fps.
